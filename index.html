How to Purchase TurboTax?

  1. Go to the official TurboTax website at turbotax.intuit.com.
  2. Select the TurboTax product that best suits your needs. TurboTax offers different versions, such as Basic, Deluxe, Premier, and Home & Business, each tailored to different tax situations.
  3. Once you’ve chosen your product, add it to your cart.
  4. During checkout, you’ll be prompted to enter your payment information.
  5. After completing your purchase, you’ll have the option to download the software directly or receive a physical CD by mail. If you choose to download, follow the instructions provided to install TurboTax on your computer.
  6. When installing TurboTax, you’ll be asked to enter your license code, which you’ll receive via email after your purchase. This code is necessary to activate the software.

Difference between TurboTax Online and TurboTax CD/Download


TurboTax Online
  • Access: Web-based, accessible from any device with internet access.
  • Cost: Pay when you file or print your return. Free to start.
  • Updates: Automatic updates managed online.
  • Storage: Returns are stored online; you should save and download your files annually.
  • Flexibility: Ideal for those who need to access their returns from multiple devices.

TurboTax CD/Download
  • Access: Installed on your computer, accessible offline.
  • Cost: Pay upfront; includes a 60-day money-back guarantee.
  • Updates: Manual updates required.
  • Storage: Returns are stored on your computer; always accessible.
  • Performance: Potentially faster performance as it doesn’t rely on internet speed.
